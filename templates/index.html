Know Your Durian King

Love durians? Unsure of what you are looking at is the durian species you want? Snap and upload a photo of them! We help you to identify them!

Note: Due the constraint of the existing durian dataset, our identification service is limited to the three most popular durian species in Singapore:

  • Mao Shan Wang (D197)
  • Sultan (D24)
  • Singapore Red Prawn (D13)
Please upload an image (.jpg or .png) of size not exceeding 3MB.

About

Bao Bao Jiak app aims to be the to-go-to smart app for you, durian lovers. We provides additional assurance on top of the existing bao jiak services from the durian merchants. We achieve this by helping you to identify your favourite durian species, using our innovative AI-powered durian recognition technology, so that you can truly enjoy and appreciate the durians with a stronger peace of mind. Educational information about common durian species sold in Singapore is available here too. Our smart, interactive and informative chat bot will help you to get the information you need and understand more about the different durian species! Don't be shy to fall in love with durians or us!
